Welcome"I'm validator!"pooled staking ^^

This system is an important component of the "I'm Panel" tool suite. It is used to provide users with a solution for validator-optimized collateral staking, which is higher than the official validator earnings of ethereum. The advantage of this system is to use the validators held by the system to provide information about Pending Orders for DEXBot, another tool we developed, The DEXBot main investment opportunities are selected from Pending Orders. And validators can provide faster transaction packaging service for DEXBot.


The economics of the "I'm Validator!" system is modeled as follows: each validator is divided into 100 shares, each worth 0.32 ETH. each validator proceeds will be distributed to investors in proportion to their equity and automatically converted into equity in DEXBot (BOT) for automatic compounding, the ratio of BOT to ETH is 10,000 to 1, a constant ratio bound by the smart contract code and cannot be changed. The robot's earnings will automatically distribute more BOTs to the investors, and the interest will be compounded automatically every day.


Users can convert BOT back to ETH at any time at a constant rate.


The profit of DEXBot is distributed in the following way: 60% of the profit of each order is distributed to BOT holders proportionally, 35% is used to reward team leaders who invite their friends to participate in DEXBot trading, and 15% will be distributed to the developer team in addition to paying Gas Fee and Nodes costs.
